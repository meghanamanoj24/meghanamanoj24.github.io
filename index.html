Project

Our project involves the development of an innovative sports app designed to create a seamless ecosystem for athletes, coaches, and shopkeepers. Through this app, athletes can sign in to find and select coaches and academies that best fit their training needs. They can also purchase sports accessories and equipment from various shopkeepers listed within the app. Coaches can connect with potential trainees and manage their training schedules, while shopkeepers can reach a targeted customer base to sell their products. This interconnected platform aims to bring all essential sports-related activities under one field, enhancing convenience and fostering a thriving sports community.
